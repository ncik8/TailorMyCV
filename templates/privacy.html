Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

When you use Rezmycv, we collect:

  • Account info — email address and password (stored securely, never shared)
  • CV & job data — the content you upload or paste, used solely to generate your tailored CV
  • Usage data — generation counts, subscription status, and basic analytics to operate and improve the Service

How We Use It

Your data is used to:

  • Deliver the CV tailoring service you signed up for
  • Enforce generation limits on your plan
  • Process payments if you subscribe to a paid plan
  • Communicate account-related notices (optional — you can unsubscribe)

Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored in Supabase (EU-west servers). Payment data is handled by Stripe — we never store your card details. We implement industry-standard encryption and access controls.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data when required by law.

Cookies

Rezmycv uses session cookies to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account and data at any time by contacting hello@rezmycv.com. Deleting your account removes all associated data within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this Policy? Email hello@rezmycv.com
